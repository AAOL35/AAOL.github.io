Welcome!

Hi guys, welcome to Arifiye Anatolian Teaching High School grads' official website.

What Do We Aim to Do?

We aim to bring together all the AAOL grads through our special events and programs to help them support each other after and during the high school years.

How?

For the above-mentioned ends, we may build a fund that will finance all needs of our members as well as our activities.
